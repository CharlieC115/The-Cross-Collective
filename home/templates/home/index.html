The Cross Collective

The Cross collective (TXC) is a ski cross training academy that specialises in ski cross training in the UK and EU. TXC was founded by Jake Richardson, multi award winning ski cross coach, in 2016 with the aim to provide high performance coaching to aspiring young athletes. TXC goals have and always will be to provide a safe and fun learning environment to increase athletes understanding of skiing movements and performance from the ages U10 through to FIS level.
